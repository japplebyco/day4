About Me

Josh Appleby is a man that wears many hats. He is a husband, father, musician, businessman, church leader, and aspiring computer programer. He has been married to his wife Laura for 11 years, he has 3 daughters; who range in age from 2 months to 4 & 1/2 years, He plays trombone, baritone, and keyboard, he works as a supervisor at Appleby & Company, and volunteers for many church activities. He is also a purveyor of interesting foods & drinks. Among his eclectic interests are; weizenbier, avant garde jazz music, and reformed theology. Josh Appleby is currently attending night school for the purpose of learning computer programming. He is a creative man that is willing to try anything, and most of the time he succeeds.
